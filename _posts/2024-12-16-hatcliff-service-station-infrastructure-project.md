## Project Scope & Deliverables

### Major Components
- **Site Preparation:** Excavations and ground leveling
- **Underground Infrastructure:** Tank farm construction and installation
- **Storage Systems:** Multiple fuel storage tanks with integrity testing
- **Forecourt Development:** Hardstanding and surface preparation
- **Fuel Distribution:** Complete pipe network layout and installation
- **Dispensing Systems:** Pump dispensers and canopy installation
- **Environmental Systems:** Effluent disposal pathway design and implementation
- **Quality Assurance:** Tank calibrations and comprehensive system testing

## Technical Highlights

### Underground Tank Farm
- Designed and supervised construction of reinforced concrete tank farm
- Implemented multiple containment systems for environmental protection
- Coordinated installation of 20,000L capacity storage tanks
- Ensured compliance with fuel storage regulations and safety standards

- **Electrical Systems:** Power distribution for pumps and lighting
- **Drainage Design:** Comprehensive effluent management system
